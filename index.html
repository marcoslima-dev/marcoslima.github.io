  • Personal Information

    My name is Marcos, nice to meet you :)

    I live in the northeast region of Brazil, I'm 19 years old, graduated in computer technician. I'm a web developer for 2 years with specialties in front-end.

  • 2018-2020

    Technical course and first contacts with development

    In 2018 I started at a technical school integrated into high school and took the technical course in informatics, where I had my first contact with programming logic and the Python language. I carried out a project where I created a game using the Construct 2 engine. I graduated in the year 2020.

    The start of my journey

    In March 2019 I was in the second year of high school, I had studied programming logic in the first year and was having contact with Python, until I decided to acquire a web development course on the Udemy platform, and that's when my career started. (My first website in the photo)

  • March 2020

    My first freelance

    In March 2020 it can be said that my career really took the first step, as it was when I participated in my first real project. I, together with 2 more friends, built an e-commerce site, I was responsible for the front-end.
